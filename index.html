FAQ

+

How much does glass installation or repair cost?

Pricing depends on the type of glass, size, and complexity of the job. Contact us with your requirements, and we’ll provide you with a free estimate. We offer competitive rates without compromising on quality.

+

How long does it take to complete a glass project?

Most projects like window repairs or glass replacements are completed within 1–3 days. Custom glass installations may take longer depending on the design and material availability. We always strive to meet your deadlines.

+

What types of glass services do you offer?

We specialize in residential and commercial glass services including window repair, glass door installation, custom mirrors, shower enclosures, storefront glass, safety glass, and more. If it’s glass, we can handle it!

+

Can you replace broken windows the same day?

In most cases, yes! We offer same-day emergency services for broken windows or glass panels. If a full replacement is not immediately possible, we’ll secure the area and schedule a prompt installation.

+

When and how do I make payments?

We require a deposit to start custom orders. The balance is usually due upon project completion. We accept cash, checks, credit cards, and online payments for your convenience.
